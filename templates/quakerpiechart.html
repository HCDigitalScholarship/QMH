Which Contributed to Friends Asylum

Information about Quaker Pie Chart

This pie chart depicts the overall percentage of contributions given by each Quaker Meeting to fund Friends Asylum. The percentages were obtained by looking at data of people who contributed from 1817-1834 and what meeting they were from. For example, the number 383 over the Philadelphia slice of the pie chart represents the fact that 383 people from some Philadelphia Meeting contributed to Friends Asylum.
